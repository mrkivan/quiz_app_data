Privacy Policy - Quiz Master - Android, Kotlin & Java

Effective date: August 18, 2025

App Version: 1.1.0

Quiz Master – Android, Kotlin & Java ("the App") is provided as a free application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Information Collection and Use

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. We only collect anonymous analytics data for app improvement:

  • Screen names visited
  • Button or feature interactions

No personal data such as name, email, or device identifiers are collected or shared.

Quiz Data

- For the first time, quiz data sets may need to be downloaded from the internet.
- Once downloaded, the data is cached locally on your device for offline use.
- This cached data is used only for gameplay and is never shared with us or third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services that collect personal information.

Children’s Privacy

Since no personal data is collected, the App is safe for all age groups.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page and will apply to the current App version.
