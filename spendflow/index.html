SpendFlow — Privacy Policy (Version 1.0)

Effective date:

Summary

SpendFlow is a personal finance app designed with privacy in mind. The app stores all income and expense data locally on your device. SpendFlow collects only anonymous analytics data to improve app stability and user experience. No personal or financial data is collected.

App features

  • Daily and monthly income tracking
  • Expense tracking by category
  • Monthly overview and balance summary
  • Charts and spending statistics
  • Optional passcode protection

Data collection

SpendFlow collects limited, anonymous analytics data only.

The app may collect:

  • Crash reports — to identify bugs and improve stability
  • Screen flow events — to understand which screens are used

What is NOT collected:

  • No income or expense data
  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone, address)
  • No account, payment, or financial information
  • No text entered into forms
  • No contacts, files, SMS, or media

Analytics data is anonymous, non-personal, and cannot be used to identify you.

Local data storage

All financial data you enter in SpendFlow is stored locally on your device only.

  • No cloud sync or remote storage
  • No servers store your financial records
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data

Security

  • Optional passcode protection to restrict app access
  • Data remains accessible only within the app

Third-party services

SpendFlow may use third-party libraries for crash reporting and analytics. These services receive only anonymous, non-personal data and do not have access to your financial information.

Children’s privacy

SpendFlow does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The app does not collect personal or financial information from any users.

Data removal

You can remove all app data by:

  • Clearing app data from device settings
  • Uninstalling the app
