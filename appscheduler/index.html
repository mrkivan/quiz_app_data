App Scheduler — Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Summary

App Scheduler is a local, on-device automation tool that helps you schedule installed apps to open at specific times. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled. In short: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on any server. Everything remains on your device.

App features

App Scheduler allows users to automate app launches on their own device. Core features include:

  • Scheduling any installed app to launch at a selected time.
  • Editing an existing schedule.
  • Cancelling a scheduled launch before it starts.
  • Supporting multiple schedules without time conflicts.
  • Automatically restoring schedules after device restart.
  • Local-only schedule records to track successful or failed launches.
  • Optional dark mode support.

Data collection — none

App Scheduler does not collect or transmit any personal data to any server.

Specifically:

  • No user information is stored outside your device.
  • No analytics, tracking SDKs, or advertising libraries are used.
  • No cloud backup or server communication is performed.
  • We do not access your contacts, messages, photos, or unrelated files.

Local storage only

All scheduling information (selected app, scheduled time, status, execution results) is stored only on your device. This data is needed to ensure the scheduler works correctly, restores schedules after reboot, and shows schedule history.

No data is synced, uploaded, or shared automatically.

Permissions used

App Scheduler requires only one mandatory permission:

  • Draw Over Other Apps — required to reliably launch scheduled apps, even when the device is locked or running a different app.

This permission is used strictly for app launch automation and nothing else.

Mandatory Notification Permission

App Scheduler also requires the Notification Permission to function properly. This permission is mandatory because:

  • The app must display a foreground service notification when scheduling or launching apps.
  • Without notification access, scheduled launches may fail on newer Android versions.
  • Notifications ensure the system does not stop or delay scheduled app launches.

The app does not send any data through notifications — they are used only to keep scheduling operations running reliably.

No cloud services, no external tracking

App Scheduler does not include:

  • Cloud sync
  • Remote servers
  • Analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reporting services
  • Third-party advertising networks

Your device never sends any schedule information to us.

Third-party libraries

App Scheduler may use open-source libraries for UI or local database storage. These libraries operate only on your device and do not send any personal data over the internet.

Children & minors

App Scheduler does not target children under 13 and does not collect any personal information.

How to remove data

You can delete all scheduling data by uninstalling the app. Uninstalling removes all locally stored files created by the app.
